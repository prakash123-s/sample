ABOUT US

Our Martial Arts Club was thundered at CIT in 2018 by Mr. Maheshwar. Prior to 2018, it was known as the Karate Club. Due to the dedication and enthusiasm for martial arts demonstrated by our members, the club was renamed to the Martial Arts Club.

Our Vision

The vision of the Martial Arts Club is to cultivate a culture of continuous improvement and leadership, empowering individuals to become confident, disciplined, and respectful members of society. By promoting the values of integrity, perseverance, and respect, we aim to inspire a new generation of martial artists who contribute to the betterment of their communities and the world.

Our Mission

The mission of the Martial Arts Club is to provide a supportive and inclusive environment where individuals of all ages and skill levels can engage in the practice and study of martial arts. Through disciplined training, we aim to promote physical fitness, mental well-being, self-defense skills, and personal growth.
